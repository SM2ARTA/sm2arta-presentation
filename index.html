What SM2ARTA does

One platform. Every event asset. End-to-end.

Streamline your events supply chain from scope and planning to delivery, reconciliation, and reporting. Eliminate manual errors, automate documentation, and give every role — managers, planners, logistics — the right view of the same live data.

Core Capabilities

Six capabilities, one platform.

Streamlined Planning

Automated workflows and an interface built for event teams — not accountants. Planners plan; the platform handles the rest.

Real-Time Analytics

Comprehensive dashboards out of the box. Connect your own BI stack via dedicated API endpoints — build any custom view your team needs.

Live Inventory

Track every asset across warehouses and venues in real time. No stock-outs, no overstock, no guesswork on bump-in day.

Effortless Teamwork

Three role-tailored interfaces for managers, planners, and logistics. Everyone sees the same truth; no spreadsheet wars.

Customizable & Scalable

Unlimited events, users, venues, and catalog items. Configure the data model yourself — no developer tickets required.

Enterprise Security

Field-level encryption, Vault-managed keys, ClamAV scanning on every upload. Built to enterprise audit standards.

Material Planning

From scope to signed-off asset list.

Build the event catalog, add venues and spaces, and let functional teams plan what they need — space by space, item by item. One consolidated view for the Material Planning Manager.

  • Unlimited users, events, venues, and catalog items
  • Per-event configuration — different scope, same platform
  • Five-minute onboarding for new functional planners
  • Planning freeze, revision history, and approval workflows
  • Excel export of the combined asset list in one click
Asset Tracking

Every asset. Every venue. Live.

From warehouse receipt to venue bump-in and back. Track deliveries, quarantine damages, and generate the paperwork that usually takes days — in seconds.

  • Real-time inventory across warehouses and venues
  • Comprehensive bump-in plans per venue
  • Automated delivery notes, inventory, and damage reports
  • Boxing and palletisation manifests on demand
  • Full audit trail on every movement and change
Beyond FF&E

Whatever you catalog, we can plan & track.

Furniture, fixtures, equipment — and everything else. Golf carts, sports gear, IT hardware, signage, consumables. If it has a SKU, it lives in SM2ARTA.

  • Any asset class, any scale — single configuration model
  • Legacy catalog reuse across multiple events
  • Bulk import from existing spreadsheets or ERP systems
  • Nomenclature system for warehouse-trackable items

Security & Compliance

Built to enterprise audit standards.

Enterprise procurement teams pass SM2ARTA without escalations. Hosted in the EU, encrypted end-to-end, audited on every change.

EU-Hosted Infrastructure

Data resident in OVH Frankfurt · ISO 27001 certified datacentre · DDoS-protected edge · Cloudflare Pro WAF.

Encryption Everywhere

TLS 1.2+ in transit · AES-256 at rest · Field-level encryption (CipherSweet / NaCl) · Keys in HashiCorp Vault.

GDPR Compliant

Data processing agreements, right-to-erasure workflows, audit exports. EU data never leaves EU.

OWASP-Hardened

Cloudflare WAF blocks OWASP Top 10 · ClamAV scans every upload · Monthly vulnerability scans · 72h critical-patch SLA.

Full Audit Trail

Every change attributed, every version recoverable. 4 monitored logging layers · real-time access logs · full traceability.

RBAC & Least Privilege

3-tier role-based access · segregation of duties · continuous access reviews · SSO & impersonation audit.
